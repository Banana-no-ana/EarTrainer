Summary

The III chord is rarely used before
the 18th century in traditional
western classical music. It is some-
times used as an inbetween to
modulate to the relative Major in
minor mode.

The III chord can also substitute as
a softer V(Dominant), exchange
the supertonic for the mediant

It's a minor triad in major key and
and augmented triad in minor key.
In minor keys, sometimes the
minor III is used instead of the
Augmented.
